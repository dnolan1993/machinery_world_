About us

Machinery World was established in 2005. We provide both adults and children with a fun day out and let them experience operating real-life construction equipment. We give you the safety information and the training you need to operate the choosen machine safely.

Services

We offer an experience day on a machine of your choice. You can choose to drive one of the following:

  • Site Dumper
  • Articulated Dumptruck
  • Skid-steer Loader
  • Telehandler
  • Loading Shovel
  • Mini-excavator
  • Excavator
  • Bulldozer

We also offer packages where you can pick multiple machines and run equipment for a full day of fun!
